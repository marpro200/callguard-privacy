# CallGuard – Privacy Policy

This repository hosts the privacy policy for **Call Guard**, an Android app that silently blocks incoming calls from numbers not saved in your contacts.

The privacy policy is published at **[privacy.profanter.dev](https://privacy.profanter.dev)**.

---

## About CallGuard

CallGuard is available on the Google Play Store at [profanter.dev.callguard](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=profanter.dev.callguard).

- No data collection
- No ads
- No analytics
- All data stays on your device
